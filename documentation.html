This model is trained to predict whether a planet is a gas giant, a super-Earth, or a Neptune-like planet. The prediction is based on the following three attributes:
- The mass of the exoplanet (measured in Jupiter's mass)
- The radius of the exoplanet (measured in Jupiter's radius)
- The orbital radius of the exoplanet (measured in AU)
